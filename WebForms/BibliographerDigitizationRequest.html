This form should be used whenever a Bibliographer or other Library staff member requests a single title or small multi-volume title for digitization. Requests will be reviewed by Preservation and if accepted, scheduled for either in-house or vendor workflows as appropriate. Preservation staff will pull the items from the collection when the form is received. Notification of completion will be made when the catalog record for the digitized item is online. Questions should be directed to pres-questions@lib.uchicago.edu.
