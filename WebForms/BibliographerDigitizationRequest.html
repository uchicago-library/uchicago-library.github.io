Bibliographer Digitization Request 

This form can be used whenever a bibliographer has a few items for digitization. Collections should be submitted via the [DCSC proposal form]. Requests will be reviewed and accepted by Kathy Arthur, the Head of Digitization, and scheduled for work as appropriate within the fiscal year. Notification of completion will be made when the catalog record for the digitized item is online. When possible, please send item(s) with a copy of the request form to Kathy Arthur, Head of Digitization, Mansueto Room 106. All questions should be directed to Kathy Arthur at kar8@uchicago.edu, or 2-8628.
