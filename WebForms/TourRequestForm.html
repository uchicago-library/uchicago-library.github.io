The University of Chicago Library is able to provide tours for groups of our campus libraries on a limited basis. Priority is given to groups affiliated with the University of Chicago. Policies for tours vary by campus library and depend on staff availability.

  • Requests must be made at least 72 hours in advance of the desired visit
  • Tours are normally available Monday-Friday from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.
  • For individuals interested in arranging for a tour for K-12 students, please view our School Group Policy

To request a tour, please complete the form below, and a Library staff member will contact you within one business day.
