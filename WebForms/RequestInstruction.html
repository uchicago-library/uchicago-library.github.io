University of Chicago librarians are happy to provide customized instruction programs for your course, highlighting relevant library resources. View examples of Library instruction programs.

To schedule a program, please complete the form below.  A librarian will respond to your request by e-mail and schedule the session.  Requests made during business hours will receive a confirmation within 24 hours. We recommend requesting your session at least one week in advance to ensure that teaching spaces are available at your preferred day/time. In some cases, we may be able to provide instruction in your classroom.

The Library also offers the option of customized course guides in addition to or in lieu of scheduled sessions.
