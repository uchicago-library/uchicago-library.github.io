The Special Collections Research Center supports the teaching mission of the University of Chicago. Each year the Special Collections Research Center hosts classes drawing on rare books, archives, and manuscripts in Special Collections.

The Special Collections Research Center welcomes a wide range of visiting groups. While our primary mission is to serve the faculty and students of the University of Chicago, we are happy to host other visiting groups as our staff time and classroom availability permits.

Instructional use of the Special Collections classrooms is reserved only for classes using Special Collections materials.

Requests are usually answered within 1-2 business days.
