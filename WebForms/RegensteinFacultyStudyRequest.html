Faculty studies are available for quarterly rental by eligible faculty and academics. See Regenstein Faculties Studies for detailed information on faculty study policies.

Fees

  • If tenured, the rental fee is $150/quarter.
  • If untenured or emeritus, the rental fee is $75/quarter.
  • There is a $50 refundable key deposit required for all new rentals.
  • We accept cash, check, VISA/Mastercard, or interdepartmental transfer as payment for faculty studies.
  • Payment is due upon rental/renewal.

Faculty Study Request Process

  • Once this form has been submitted, the ID & Privileges Office will verify eligibility and respond with available study options within two business days.
  • The study key will be available at the ID & Privileges Office in the first floor lobby of Regenstein once payment has been received.
  • Questions? Call the ID & Privileges Office at 773-702-3344 or email studies@lib.uchicago.edu.
