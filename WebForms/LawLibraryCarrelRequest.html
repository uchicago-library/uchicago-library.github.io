The Library has a limited number of assigned carrels on the fourth, fifth and sixth floors for use by research assistants, law students in seminar courses, students working on law journals, and others with special research needs. Carrels are assigned quarterly. Most library materials, including non-circulating items, may be checked out to carrels.
